                    Dataset Card for SP500-EDGAR-10K

                    Dataset Summary

                    This dataset contains the annual reports for all SP500 historical constituents from 2010-2022 from SEC EDGAR Form 10-K filings. It also contains n-day future returns of each firm's stock price from each filing date.
