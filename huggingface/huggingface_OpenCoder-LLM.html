      The OpenCoder dataset is composed of the following datasets:

      opc-fineweb-code-corpus

      This code-related data from Fineweb was specifically used in OpenCoder pre-training. We employ fastText in three iterative rounds to recall a final dataset of 55B code and math-related data. You can find math-related data at OpenCoder-LLM/fineweb-math-corpus.
