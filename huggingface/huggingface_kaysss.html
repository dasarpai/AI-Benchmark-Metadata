          Dataset Card for Medicines Data

          • This dataset contains information about various diseases, prescription medicines, their prices, manufacturers, and other relevant details scraped from an online pharmacy website. It provides structured insights into different medications, including their availability, prescription requirements, manufacturers, active ingredients, and potential side effects.
          • The dataset also contains introduction and detailed description of the medicine, including usage, mechanism of action, warnings, side effects, and interactions.

          Dataset Details

          Data Fields

          1. disease_name (string): Name of the disease the medication is used for.
          2. disease_url (string): Link to the disease page for additional information.
          3. med_name (string): Name of the medicine.
          4. med_url (string): Link to the medicine's product page.
          5. final_price (string): Final selling price of the medication.
          6. price (string): MRP and any discount percentage if applicable.
          7. prescription_required (string): Indicates whether a prescription is required (e.g., "Rx required").
          8. drug_variant (string): Variant type, e.g., number of tablets per strip.
          9. drug_manufacturer (string): Name of the manufacturer.
          10. drug_manufacturer_origin (string): Country of origin of the manufacturer.
          11. drug_content (string): Introduction and detailed description of the medicine, including usage, mechanism of action, warnings, side effects, and interactions.
          12. generic_name (string): Generic name of the active ingredient(s).
          13. img_urls (list of strings): URLs of images of the medicine.

          Uses

          • Building recommendation systems for healthcare professionals.
          • Researching common medications and their side effects.
          • Understanding drug availability and manufacturer distribution.
          • Price comparison analysis of medicines.
          • Analyzing trends in prescription medicine requirements.
