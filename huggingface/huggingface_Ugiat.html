              NERCat Dataset

              Dataset Summary

              The NERCat dataset is a manually annotated collection of Catalan-language television transcriptions, designed to improve Named Entity Recognition (NER) performance for the Catalan language. The dataset covers diverse domains such as politics, sports, and culture, and includes 9,242 sentences with 13,732 named entities annotated across eight categories: Person, Facility, Organization, Location, Product, Event, Date, and Law. The dataset was created to address the lack of high-quality annotated data for Catalan, a low-resource language, and to support the development of NLP applications in Catalan media, governance, and cultural domains.

              Languages

              The dataset primarily contains Catalan (ca) language text, with occasional Spanish (es) code-switching segments. The language is represented in its standard form, with no specific regional or dialectal variations highlighted.

              Label Descriptions

              • Person: Names of individuals, including fictional and historical figures.

              • Facility: Physical infrastructures such as airports, bridges, and stadiums.

              • Organization: Entities such as companies, institutions, political parties, and media outlets.

              • Location: Geographical places, including cities, regions, and landmarks. This also includes names of countries, mountain lakes, or planets.

              • Product: Commercial products, such as branded phones or soft drinks. This category also includes cultural products, such as the titles of films, books, and songs.

              • Event: Named occurrences like festivals, elections, or historical battles.

              • Date: Temporal expressions indicating specific days, months, years, or time periods.

              • Law: Names of laws, legal frameworks, and judicial matters, including constitutions, court rulings, and high-profile judicial cases.

              Explanation of the Fields:

              • tokenized_text: This is the sentence broken down into individual tokens (words).

              • ner: This is a list of tuples, where each tuple represents a named entity in the sentence:

                • The first and second elements represent the token indices in the tokenized_text list (e.g., [1, 3] means the entity spans tokens at positions 1 to 3).
                • The third element is the label for that entity.

              Annotations

              All the processes involved in creating the dataset, including data collection and annotation, were carried out manually by experts.

              Supported Tasks

              Named Entity Recognition (NER): The dataset can be used to train and evaluate models for Named Entity Recognition in Catalan. Success on this task is typically measured by achieving high precision, recall, and F1-scores. The fine-tuned NERCat model achieves near-perfect scores in categories like Person, Organization, and Location, with significant improvements in underrepresented categories such as Facility, Product, and Law.

              • Metrics: Precision, Recall, F1-score
              • Model: NERCat Fine-Tuned GLiNER Model
