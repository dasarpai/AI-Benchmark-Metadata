CMP Facade Database

We present a dataset of facade images assembled at the Center for Machine Perception, which includes 606 rectified images of facades from various sources, which have been manually annotated. The facades are from different cities around the world and diverse architectural styles. Documentation

Data origin, format and processing, annotation principles for 12 classes are specified in the report.
