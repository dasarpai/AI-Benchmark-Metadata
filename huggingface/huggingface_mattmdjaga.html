                              Dataset Card for Human parsing data (ATR)

                              Dataset Summary

                              This dataset has 17,706 images and mask pairs. It is just a copy of Deep Human Parsing ATR dataset. The mask labels are: "0": "Background", "1": "Hat", "2": "Hair", "3": "Sunglasses", "4": "Upper-clothes", "5": "Skirt", "6": "Pants", "7": "Dress", "8": "Belt", "9": "Left-shoe", "10": "Right-shoe", "11": "Face", "12": "Left-leg", "13": "Right-leg", "14": "Left-arm", "15": "Right-arm", "16": "Bag", "17": "Scarf"
