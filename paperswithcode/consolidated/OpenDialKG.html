OpenDialKG

Introduced by Moon et al. in OpenDialKG: Explainable Conversational Reasoning with Attention-based Walks over Knowledge Graphs

OpenDialKG contains utterance from 15K human-to-human role-playing dialogs is manually annotated with ground-truth reference to corresponding entities and paths from a large-scale KG with 1M+ facts.

Source: OpenDialKG: Explainable Conversational Reasoning with Attention-based Walks over Knowledge Graphs
